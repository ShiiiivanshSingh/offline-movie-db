# shelf!

Shelf is a personal movie collection that helps you keep track of the films you own or want to watch. You can search your library, mark movies as watched, add new titles, change posters, and remove entries whenever you like. It also lets you import and export your collection so you can back it up or move it to another device.

* **Search Movies** - Find movies instantly by typing in the search bar.
* **Mark as Watched** - Click a poster to mark a movie as watched or unwatched.
* **Sort Movies** - Sort your collection by name, year, or watched status.
* **Add Movies** - Add new movies to your collection.
* **Change Posters** - Set a custom poster for any movie.
* **Remove Movies** - Delete individual movies from your collection.
* **Export Library** - Save your collection as a backup file.
* **Import Library** - Restore your collection from a backup file.
* **Open on Letterboxd** - Click a movie title to search it on Letterboxd.
* **Auto Save** - Your collection is saved automatically.
